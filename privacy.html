Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how the RWECS app handles information.

Information Collection

RWECS does not require account creation and does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

Training data (such as session duration, distance, and route) is generated during active sessions and stored locally on the device.

Location Data

Location data is used only during an active training session to:

  • Record distance and route
  • Allow the session to continue recording when the app is in the background (for example, when the phone is in a pocket or the screen is locked)

Location data is not collected or used outside of active sessions.

Bluetooth

Bluetooth is used only to connect optional external sensors, such as RWECS wearable devices and heart rate monitors, to enhance training metrics.

The app functions without external sensors.

Apple Watch

The Apple Watch app can record sessions independently, without the iPhone nearby. Session data is synchronized with the iPhone when available.

Data Sharing

RWECS does not sell, share, or transmit personal data to third parties.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the app. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us at: support@rwecs.app

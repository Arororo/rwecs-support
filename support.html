RWECS App Support

RWECS is a walking and race walking training app for iPhone and Apple Watch. The app can record sessions independently and can optionally connect to external sensors for advanced training metrics.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an RWECS device to use the app?
No. Sessions can be recorded without any external sensors. RWECS wearable devices provide additional metrics such as Loss of Contact feedback.

Why does the app request Bluetooth access?
Bluetooth is used to connect optional external sensors, such as RWECS devices and heart rate monitors, to enhance training metrics.

Why does the app use location services?
Location services are used during an active session to record distance and route, and to allow the session to continue recording when the phone is in a pocket or when the screen is locked.

Does the Apple Watch app work without the iPhone nearby?
Yes. The Apple Watch app can record sessions independently, without the iPhone nearby.
